About

Hi, I'm Abdelrahman Mohamed — a passionate Flutter developer with a strong focus on building clean, responsive, and scalable mobile applications. I recently graduated with a degree in Computer & Management Sciences, and I'm actively seeking internship or junior developer roles to grow my experience in real-world development. I enjoy turning creative ideas into functional apps using Flutter, Firebase, and modern development principles like Clean Architecture and BLoC state management. I've built several full-featured applications, including e-commerce apps, real-time chat, and book discovery tools. My strengths include problem-solving, UI/UX design awareness, clean code practices, and a strong commitment to continuous learning. I'm always exploring new technologies and looking to join a team where I can contribute and grow as a developer. Let’s build something amazing together!
